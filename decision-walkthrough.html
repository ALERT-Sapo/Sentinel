Scope of practice reminderPulseless rhythms require immediate CPR + code blue activation simultaneously. For unstable V-tach with a pulse, floor nurses are credentialed to perform synchronized cardioversion without a provider present. If the patient is awake, defer to the acute response team for sedation decision.
