⚠️
Tachyarrhythmia — scope reminderPulseless rhythms require immediate CPR + code blue activation simultaneously. For unstable V-tach with a pulse, floor nurses are credentialed to perform synchronized cardioversion without a provider present. If the patient is awake, defer to the acute response team for sedation decision.
⚠️
Bradyarrhythmia — scope reminderTranscutaneous pacing (TCP) is a provider-initiated intervention. Nursing role is anterior-posterior pad placement and patient preparation. Do not initiate pacing without the team present.
